How to Play:

1. Select your character.

2. Choose an enemy to fight.

3. If you can defeat 3 enemies in a row, you win!

HINT: Characters with high health tend to have weaker attacks - and vice versa. However, your attack increases each time you fight, so choose the order of your enemies wisely!
